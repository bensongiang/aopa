My Practice

What makes my practice unique is my devotion and dedication to my patients in helping them heal through a combination of multiple Traditional Chinese Medicine procedures and techniques (these procedures and techniques can be seen in the 'Services' section of the site).

I realized from my own clinical experience that stress is such an important factor in creating/ increasing imbalances in our body that end up becoming a major part of the underlying cause of our physical problems. Part of my work is to help patients identify the stress factors that are leading to their physical pain.

For patients with a lot of stress or who are depressed, I spend more private time with them to communicate how to best address their stress and clinical needs. My medicinal experience has shown me the importance of having a mental and physical balance, which is why I tailor my treatments to best support patients in achieving a healthy balance, and so far it's proven to be very effective.

The human body has a powerful natural healing ability. In many situations, all that is needed to is to provide our patients with the best possible mental and physical support to evoke and trigger their body's natural ability to heal. That being said, for pain coming from injuries, the earlier patients start their treatment with me, the faster the healing is and the better the results are in recovering.

For patients with cancer, my treatments help relieve their pain so that they can get out of bed, exercise, increase their appetite with better digestive function, and overall improve their energy (Qi) and their mood and/or depression. From reducing their pain, most of my patients are able to restore some activities in getting back to their daily routines.

Some of my patients will continue to come back for acupuncture and treatment even after their major issues have been resolved to keep their body balanced as a preventative tool to prevent future pain/injuries/stress, and to live a healthier life in general.

We all know that if you buy a new car and take it in for regular maintenance, the car normally lasts longer and has less problems in its lifetime. The same concept applies to the human body! For most people, incorporating acupuncture as part of their health maintenance routine can be very beneficial in living a healthier and longer life.

The majority of my treatment sessions last between an hour and an hour and a half, depending on each patient’s respective needs at their time of visit. For new patients, the first visit is generally over one hour.
